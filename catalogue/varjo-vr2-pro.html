    Varjo VR-2 Pro

    Headset features:

    • Room-scale external tracking compatible with Steam VR™ and ART™
    • Bionic Display™: combines two 1920 x 1080 low persistence micro-OLEDs and two 1440 x 1600 low persistence AMOLEDs ("at over 20/20 vision").
    • Industrial-grade, sub-degree accuracy integrated 100hz stereo eye tracking
    • Integrated Ultraleap hand tracking.
    • 60/90 Hz refresh rate
    • 87° field of view
    • 605g (+ headband) + counterweight 250g weight
    More information here.

    SteamVR-capable base stations and controllers are not included. For audio, external headphones are required.
    Note: AMD GPUs are not currently supported by Varjo.
